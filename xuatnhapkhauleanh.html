About XuatNhapKhauLeAnh

Established in 2016, Xuất Nhập Khẩu Lê Ánh is Vietnam's top institute providing real-world IE education with 9 facilities in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City. Over 10,000 graduates now contribute to blue-chip multinationals like Honda, Opec Plastics, Nghi Son Refinery and major Vietnamese trade enterprises.

Le Anh focuses considerable investments continually enhancing its rigorous curriculum, elite teaching faculty, cutting-edge facilities and student support teams - cementing its reputation for quality and seriousness benefiting individuals and industry alike.

Practical IE Course Models

With classes requiring substantial real practice under instructor guidance plus companies needing hundreds trained, Le Anh offers onsite and in-center options:

  • Onsite Classes: Expert coaches directly teach at company premises tailoring topics suiting business activities for flexibility and customization - ideal for larger groups.

  • In-Center: Students attend regular public classes at Le Anh’s modern equipped facilities in case of smaller trainee numbers or staggered staff scheduling. Multiple intakes occur monthly in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City facilitating anytime enrollment.

The harmonized approaches enable all organizations to access Le Anh’s industry-leading IE course.
